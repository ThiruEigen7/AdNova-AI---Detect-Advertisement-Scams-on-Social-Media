About AdNova AI

AdSentry AI is an advanced tool designed to detect and prevent scam advertisements on social media platforms. Our AI-powered solution analyzes ad content, images, and videos to protect users from fraudulent activities.

Features of AdNova AI

Advanced AI Technology to detect fraudulent advertisements and protect users from cyber scams.

Advertisement Scam Detection

Detect fraudulent advertisements by analyzing ad content, images, and videos to protect users from scams.

User Profiling

Analyze user behavior to identify potential risks and protect vulnerable user groups from targeted scams.

Phishing Link Detection

Identify and block phishing links in ads to prevent users from falling victim to malicious websites.

Real-Time Detection

Monitor and notify users about potential scam ads in real-time to ensure immediate protection.

How to Use AdNova AI

Step 1

Paste the URL of the advertisement you want to check.

Step 2

Click the "Detect" button to analyze the ad.

Step 3

View the results to see if the ad is a scam or not.
